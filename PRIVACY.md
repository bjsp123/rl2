# Privacy Policy — rl2

**Effective date:** 8 July 2026
**Developer:** Pineapple House
**Contact:** bjsp123gpc@gmail.com

This privacy policy applies to **rl2** (working title), a single-player roguelike
game for Android and desktop published by Pineapple House ("we", "us").

## Summary

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely
on your device.

## Data we collect

**None.** The game does not collect, transmit, or share:

- personal information (name, email, contacts, identifiers)
- location data
- usage analytics or telemetry
- crash reports
- advertising identifiers

The game contains no advertising, no third-party analytics SDKs, no user
accounts, and no online multiplayer. It does not require an internet
connection to play.

## Data stored on your device

The game stores save files, settings, and local records (such as your hall of
fame) on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is removed
when you uninstall the game.

## In-app purchases

If a version of the game offers in-app purchases, payment is processed
entirely by **Google Play Billing**. We never receive or store your payment
details (card numbers, billing address, etc.). Google's handling of your
payment information is governed by the
[Google Play Terms of Service](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/) and
the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). The only
information the game itself uses is whether a purchase has been made, which is
stored on your device to unlock the purchased content.

## Permissions

The game requests no sensitive Android permissions. It does not access your
location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or files outside its own
storage area.

## Children

The game does not collect data from anyone, including children. Its content
rating is provided through the IARC questionnaire on Google Play.

## Changes to this policy

If a future update changes what data the game handles (for example, adding
opt-in crash reporting), we will update this policy and the effective date
above before that update is released. The current version of this policy is
always available at this URL.
